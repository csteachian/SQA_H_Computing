Food & drink at Paradise

In need of refreshment? With our great golf course and an awesome amusements area, Paradise comes equipped with our very own putt-tastic diner.

You'll find a tempting range of food to keep everyone happy, from nacho sharers and hot dogs to yummy 16" pizzas. We also have a kid's menu for those little putters!

We also have a variety of refreshing drinks available, including: ice-cold slushies, tempting milkshakes and soft and hot drinks.

So whether you're all putted out or re-fuelling before your next game, why not take a trip to our diner and enjoy a well-earned treat.
